Republicans have also cheated at confirming nominations to the lower level federal courts.

We have here a visualization of the judicial confirmations for each two year session of congress. Each dot is a presidential nomination, and the green dots are nominations confirmed by the Senate.

Notice how in the last two years of Obama's term (2015-2016), the Republican controlled Senate confirmed an unprecedently low number (both in absolute terms and in relative terms) of Obama's judicial nominees, leaving a large number of lifetime appointments for Trump to fill.
